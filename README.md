# Car-bin
A trash can for cars which is easy to assemble and can even store liquids

Have you ever wanted to throw out your trash while in a long car ride?, well this is the simple solution. A portable (easily removable) trash can which you can store in your car and stores trash, even liquids instead of having the problem of holding it until you reach a stop
I have made this car dustbin for the convinience of the users, which solves my main issue with long car drives.

Assembly: 
1. Pass the tube through the bin and through the lid (make sure that the lid is inbetween the bin).
2. Lock the tube by placing the tube cover and locking it with a string
3. Step 3 sand it  (optional)

Print settings: 
Support as well as outer brim in turned on
